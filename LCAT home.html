Director’s Message

In the age of advanced technology professional education has become essential. But the students living in the backward areas fail to get facility primarily due to lack of exposure. That basically is the reason our students are not able to get mileage in the present context. Keeping this in mind our organization has opened Lokadrusti College of Advanced Technology (LCAT) at our Godramuda Campus near Khanar. I would be extremely happy if more students will enroll themselves from the rural areas of KBK districts. I sincerely believe, this College will become a platform for dream fulfillment for our students.

Mr. Abani Mohan Panigrahi

Principal's Message

It is my proud privilege to welcome you all to Lokadrusti College Of Advanced Technology. This Institute has been playing a pivotal role in shaping the careers of aspiring students through creation of generations of thinkers & dreamers of tomorrow. The environment of learning in this campus is conducive to developing a Scientifically & technically strong community with high ethical and moral values. I invite you to join this temple of learning and fulfill our endeavor to let you hitch your wagon to the stars.

Mr. Tapas Ranjan Panigrahi
